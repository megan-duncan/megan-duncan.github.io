My Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal is to have more flexibility in my work and the ability to work from home. I want to have more balance in my life and being able to change my timetable when needed will help this. As for a career pathway, I am still unsure of where I want to end up, I am hoping that going through Dev will give me more of an idea.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

A strength of mine in learning, is that I WANT to learn. When I am interested in the topic I am learning, I put my all in.

Weaknesses: I can be easily distracted, if my mind wanders, it can sometimes be hard for me to come back. To combat this, I have been setting a Pomodoro focus timer (that also doubles as a plant growing game) to make sure that when I am in my study zone, I stay there.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

Though I have spent a lot of time working in teams, I have not had much experience working on group projects. I am hoping to come out of Dev able to work with a range of people and have some strategies for when things arenâ€™t going to plan.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme.

I have made myself a timetable for foundations so I know when I am studying and when I am able to relax, this helps me to commit to being productive, because if I stop studying I know I will just have to do more at another time and cut into my free time.

I am using a Pomodoro timer, which means I am definitely getting some breaks to stretch my legs and rest my brain. This also means that I am more productive when I am learning because it is for just set times. I am also making sure I give myself down time in the evenings to read a book or go for a walk. I know it is important for me to have the time away from screens to keep myself from getting overwhelmed.

When things are starting to get to me, I will talk through what is happening with my partner. This always helps me to work out what I need to do or change to make me feel more in control.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

When I need help with content, I will first ask in the slack channel, to see if any of my peers can help me. If it is something that I believe is a facilitator issue, I will contact Joseph to help.

I also have a friend who has been through Dev and has offered her help with any issues I have, so if I feel it is something she could help with, I will contact her.

A description of what you expect from the facilitation team

I expect that the facilitation team will either be able to help when I am stuck, or will be able to point me in the right direction if it is something I need to find myself. I also hope that if things appear to be overwhelming myself or any of my peers that they will support that person and assist them in getting the help they need.

Any scheduling information such as block-out times when you are committed to other things.

During foundations I am working Weds and Thurs from 4pm to 8pm. Outside of this time I am focused on my study. I will be finishing at work the week before bootcamp so I will be able to focus entirely on my study. Outside of work, I have scheduled my study time in, to make my time as productive as I can.
