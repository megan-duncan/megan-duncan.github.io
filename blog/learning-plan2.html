My Bootcamp Learning Plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

My long term goal is to have more flexibility in my work and the ability to work from home. I want to have more balance in my life and being able to change my timetable when needed will help this. As for a career pathway, I am still unsure of where I want to end up, but I want to work in a company with a strong team culture.

A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

A strength of mine in learning, is that I WANT to learn. When I am interested in the topic I am learning, I put my all in.

Limitations: I can be easily distracted, if my mind wanders, it can sometimes be hard for me to come back. To combat this, I have been setting a Pomodoro focus timer.

During foundations I have found it really challenging to ask for help and to let myself be wrong, this is going to make bootcamp really challenging, though perhaps once I am in the same room as everyone I will find it easier to ask for help, as I will be able to see others doing the same.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Speaking in front of the group will be super challenging for me until I get to know them. When I am unsure of my abilities, I find it difficult to speak to groups.

I will also find it challenging to stay motivated for 9 weeks, and staying on top of my mental health can be hard while Im busy, so that will be something I will work on.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

Though I have spent a lot of time working in teams, I have not had much experience working on group projects. I am hoping to come out of Dev able to work with a range of people and have some strategies for when things arenâ€™t going to plan.

I would also like to become better at reflections and keeping a record of my learning.

What are my expectations of the bootcamp team?

I expect that the bootcamp team will either be able to help when I am stuck, or will be able to point me in the right direction if it is something I need to find myself. I also hope that if things appear to be overwhelming myself or any of my peers that they will support that person and assist them in getting the help they need.

What are your expactations of yourself on bootcamp?

I expect to work productively and complete all of my work by the dates I am meant to.

I expect myself to communicate well with my teachers, peers and any others I come in contact with.

I will be using a Pomodoro timer, which means I will be getting some breaks to stretch my legs and rest my brain. This also means that I can be more productive when I am learning because it is for just set times.

To stay sane, I will set myself time in the evenings to read a book or go for a walk. I know it is important for me to have the time away from screens to keep myself from getting overwhelmed.

When I need help with content, I will first ask my peers, as chances are, they are working through similar issues. If it is something that needs more help, I will ask one of my teachers.
